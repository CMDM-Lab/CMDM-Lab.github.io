IDENTITY
Theme Version: 2.1.2
Author: Michael Van Den Berg 
Author URL: http://michaelvandenberg.com/

--------------------
=== Description ===
--------------------

Identity is a modern, bright and colorful tumblog style theme designed for the many bloggers who are young of heart, mind and/or spirit. Identity supports all post formats (standard, aside, image, video, audio, gallery, quote, link, status) and in the header you can display your gravatar image and users description.

=== Installation ===
--------------------

1. Sign into your WordPress dashboard, go to Appearance > Themes, and click Add New.
2. Click Add New.
3. Click Upload.
4. Click Choose File and select the theme zip file.
5. Click Install Now.
6. Click Add New, then click Upload, then click Choose File.
7. After WordPress installs the theme, click Activate.
8. You've successfully installed your new theme!

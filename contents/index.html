Our research group focuses on Computational Molecular Design and Biomarker Detection in Metabolomics, which involves the development of new algorithms and tools to identify and design molecular structures for the targeted use.

Lab focus

Computational Molecular Design:

  • Computer-aided drug design
  • A.I. for drug discovery

Metabolomics:

  • Biomarker detection
  • Precision medicine
